# How to Sell This to Clinics

## Simple pitch

ClinicCall AI is a 24/7 AI receptionist that answers clinic calls, gives timing and location details, collects appointment requests, and saves call summaries for staff.

## What to promise

- 24/7 call answering
- Fewer missed calls
- Appointment request collection
- Call summaries
- Emergency redirection
- Affordable monthly plan

## What not to promise

- Do not promise diagnosis
- Do not promise medical advice
- Do not promise replacement of doctors
- Do not claim guaranteed appointment confirmation without clinic staff approval

## Suggested pricing in India

- Basic: ₹2,999/month
- Standard: ₹5,999/month
- Premium: ₹9,999/month

## Demo script

Call the Twilio number and say:

“Hi, I want to book an appointment tomorrow evening. My name is Rahul and I have tooth pain.”

Then open dashboard and show the appointment request.
